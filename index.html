The Source Foundation

Learn from the source

We're a global network dedicated to peer-to-peer learning.

The Source Foundation is changing the future of entrepreneurship support by developing and syndicating peer-to-peer learning formats. The Source Foundation initiatives span 4 continents, and include a network of more than 50 leaders and 3,000 entrepreneurs . We build startup communities, and develop new, more practical ways of sharing knowledge in a fast-changing global environment.

What We Do

  • Leancamp

    Connecting the world's leading Lean, Agile and Design-led businesses.

    Leancamp is a grassroots startup conference that's run in 15 cities around the world.

  • Village Accelerator

    An accelerator for the world's most unlikely tech founders.

    Village Accelerator is an open-source, in-person training programme run with Roma people in Bulgaria, designed to be syndicated to The Next Billion.

  • Starter Pack

    What would they do if they were just starting out today?

    Starter Pack is a blog that interviews a diverse range of accomplished professionals about starting out.

  • The Sources

    Lessons from entrepreneurs on the same path.

    The Sources is an online course for African startups that shares practical advice on Africa's unique environment.

  • Source Summit

    Source Summit is an inter-disciplinary conference, designed to progress state-of-the-art approaches through sharing practical and current challenges.
